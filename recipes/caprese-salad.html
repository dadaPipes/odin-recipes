Caprese Salad

Description

This will take you through how to create a delicious Caprese Salad

Step by step, from scratch to the finish line

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe tomatoes
  • 125g of fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze (optional)

Steps

  1. Slice the tomatoes and fresh mozzarella cheese into 1/4-inch thick slices
  2. Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices on a serving platter, alternating them and slightly overlapping
  3. Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices
  4. Drizzle extra-virgin olive oil over the salad
  5. If desired, drizzle a bit of balsamic glaze over the top for extra flavor
  6. Season with a pinch of salt and freshly ground black pepper
  7. Serve immediately as a refreshing appetizer or side dish
