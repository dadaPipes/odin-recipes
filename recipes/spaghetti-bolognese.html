Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

This will take you through how to create a delicious Spaghetti Bolognese

Step by step, from scratch to the finish line

Ingredients

  • 200 g of ground beef
  • 200 g of spaghetti
  • 1 can (400g) of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat and add a bit of olive oil
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pan. Saut√© until they become fragrant and translucent
  3. Add the ground beef to the pan and cook until it's browned and crumbled
  4. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and let the sauce simmer for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  5. While the sauce simmers, cook the spaghetti in boiling water according to package instructions
  6. Once the spaghetti is cooked al dente, drain it and serve it with the Bolognese sauce on top
  7. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves if desired
